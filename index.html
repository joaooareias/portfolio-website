    I'm João Areias, a 26 years old Software Engineer with a passion for technology and challenges. I was born in Porto and raised in Mirandela, a small town in the district of Bragança. At the age of 17 I moved back to Porto, where I had the privilege of pursuing my academic path.
    My journey began with a curricular internship in order to finish my master's degree in software engineering, and since then I've worked in several areas, with a greater focus on web development. Some of my greatest achievements include having been part of a team working on a product that is used by major brands, and having successfully developed and delivered applications for a client in the financial sector.
    Aside from my work, I'm passionate about movies and video games, and something I value a lot is spending quality time with those I love the most. Something that has caught my interest more recently is cooking and putting together legos/puzzles.
    I consider myself to be a fun person who is always ready to embrace new challenges, proactive and who loves learning new things and challenging myself.
